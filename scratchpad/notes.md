# Job Description: Senior AI Engineer - Founding Team
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About this role
### Who this is NOT for -
    - sm1 wanting well-scoped role with defined ladder (Google, Meta, etc)
    - sm1 bouncing b/w early-stage startups
    - want to just code without thinking abt business stuff
    - Spent career in pune research env (academic labs, research-only roles)
    - Only recent (under 12 months) AI Experience of calling OpenAI 
        - **Exception:** ancient pre-LLM-era experience (mainly retrieval and ranking)
    - Senior engineer not written production code in last 18 months
        - due to reasons like migration to "architecture" or "tech lead" roles

### Who this is for -
    - Simultaneously confortable with contradictory things 
        - sm1 who is somewhat both a Shipper and a Researcher
        - More Shipper than Researcher btw
    - Deep technical depth in modern ML systems
        - embeddings, retrieval, ranking, LLMs, fine-tuning.
    - Scappy product-engineer attritude 
        1. ship somewhat working ranker fast
        2. learn what to do from real users
        3. optimize according to needs
    - Generally "Senior Engineer"-like with about same exp as 5-9 years

## Skills needed
### MUST HAVE
    - Production exp with embedding-based retrieval systems
        - sentence-transformers, openai embeddings, bge, e5 or         
        - handled embedding drift, index refresh, retrieval quality regression
        - model doesnt matter, production exp does
    - Production exp with vector databases or hybrid search infrastructure
        - pinecone, weaviate, qdrant, milvus, opensearch, elasticsearch, FAISS, etc
        - specifics doesnt matter, operation exp does
    - Strong python (good code quality)
    - Hands-on exp designing evaluation frameworks for ranking systems
        - NDCG, MRR, MAP, offline-to-online correlation, A/B test interpretation

### Good if have
    - LLM fine-tuning exp (LoRA, QLoRA, PEFT)
    - Experience with learning-to-rank models (XGBoost-based or neural)
    - Prior exposure to HR-tecg, recruiting techs, or marketplace products
    - Background in distributed systems or large-scale inference optimization
    - Open-source contributions in AI/ML space

### DO NOT WANT (Rejection Criteria)
    - Title chaser - hops companies for bigger titles in 1.5 years or so
    - Framework enthusiasts - (just read the docx)
    - People who have ONLY worked at consulting firms -
        - TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Accenture, Cognizant, Capgemini, etc
        - If have prior product-company exp "thats fine"
    - People with primary expertise in CV, Speech or robotics -
        - without significant NLP/IR exposure.
    - People whose work has veen fully closed source proprietary systems for 5+ years 
        - without external validation (papers, talks, open-source)
